# NVIDIA DeepBench (Normal)

The _normal_ DeepBench (CUDA FP16/FP32) suite includes:

- **conv_bench** – Convolutional layers benchmark (CUDA)
	 
- **gemm_bench** – GEMM benchmark (CUDA)
	 
- **rnn_bench** – RNN (LSTM/GRU) benchmark (CUDA)
